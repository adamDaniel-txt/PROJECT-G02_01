Kafe Tiga Belas

A family-owned business, is a charming establishment offering a delightful combination of aromatic coffee and delectable cakes. With a passionate team of five siblings at the helm, this cozy cafe creates an inviting ambiance for coffee enthusiasts and dessert lovers alike. Tiga Belas Cafe takes pride in sourcing the finest coffee beans, ensuring each cup is a rich and flavorful experience. From classic espresso-based beverages to handcrafted specialty brews, there's something to satisfy every palate. Complementing the coffee selection are tantalizing cakes, baked with love and skill, catering to a variety of tastes. Tiga Belas Cafe is your perfect destination for a cozy indulgence in quality coffee and delightful desserts.
